Fried Chicken

About our Fried Chicken

Our fried chicken is golden, crispy, and full of bold flavor. Each piece is seasoned with a special blend of herbs and spices, then fried to perfection for a crunchy outside and juicy, tender inside.

We use fresh, high-quality chicken that's marinated for deep flavor in every bite. Whether you choose wings, drumsticks, or fillets, our fried chicken delivers the perfect mix of crunch, spice, and satisfaction..

ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Flour
  • Seasoning/Spices
  • Oil

Cooking steps

  1. Season chicken
    2. - Marinate or coat chicken with spices and herbs.
  2. Dredge in flour
    3. - Coat each piece evenly with seasoned flour.
  3. Heat oil
    4. - Preheat oil in a deep pan or fryer to about 175°C (350°F).
  4. Fry chicken
    5. - Fry until golden brown and cooked through (about 10–15 mins).
